Overwatch

Overwatch, it is an fps where you try to move the payload or capture a point to win. It has 23 heroes to pick from which have alot of different personality and playstyles. My favorites (mains) are Genji, Mei, Reinhardt and Zenyatta. One from each category!

There are 4 categories/classes: Healer/Support, Offense/Dps (Damage Per Second), Defense and Tank.

Support

Support heroes can heal allies, enhance their team's abilities, and debuff enemies. They are poor duelists and perform best with their team around them.
